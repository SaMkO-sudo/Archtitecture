Personal Information

Mark Livuw is a talented Enterier Designer known for his innovative and contemporary approach to creating functional and stylish living spaces. With a keen eye for detail and a strong sense of aesthetics, Mark seamlessly blends color, texture, and layout to transform ordinary interiors into visually stunning environments. His work often reflects a balance between modern minimalism and cozy sophistication, tailored to each client’s unique lifestyle and preferences. Whether designing a chic urban apartment or a serene family home, Mark Livuw consistently delivers spaces that are not only beautiful but also deeply personal and inviting.

Mark Livuw has recently been honored with the prestigious “Best Interior Designer of the Year” award, a testament to his outstanding vision and commitment to elevating living spaces.
